Healthcare cyberattacks are on the rise

The number of cyberattacks is on the rise and healthcare organizations are the prime target. As data becomes the new healthcare currency, protecting it will be key. Rapid transformations in clinical innovations, connected medical devices, and market complexity have amplified the need for its protection.

The aftermath of a cyberattack can have devastating impacts to patients and healthcare organizations including regulatory fines, reputational damage, lawsuits, and patient mortality.
